Outception is a **live news wall**.

A public, login-free feed aggregates stories from 2,500+ sources so anyone can
keep up with what's happening across the ecosystem in one place, laid out in
topic columns and readable on web and mobile.

- **Open news feed.** A fast, public news wall; no account required to read.
- **Ad-supported.** Free to read, monetized with Google ads (AdMob on mobile,
  AdSense on web).
- **Open source.** Built for transparency and community contributions.
