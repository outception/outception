Outception is a **live news wall with pay-to-promote**.

A public, login-free feed aggregates stories from 250+ sources so anyone can
keep up with what's happening across the ecosystem in one place. On top of that
sits a paid promotion layer: logged-in users can pay to feature their own
content as a "Promoted" card in the wall, via a simple FIFO featured-slot queue
per news category.

- **Open news feed.** A fast, public news wall — no account required to read.
- **Pay-to-promote.** Login-gated paid promotions surface as in-feed
  "Promoted" cards. Payments are handled by an external payment provider.
- **Open source.** Built for transparency and community contributions.
