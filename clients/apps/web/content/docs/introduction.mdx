## What is Outception?

Outception is a **live news wall** aggregating headlines from 2,500+ sources
across every topic. The public news API is open and free to read.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Live News Feed" icon="newspaper">
    A continuously updated wall of headlines aggregated from 2,500+ sources,
    searchable and grouped by category.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
