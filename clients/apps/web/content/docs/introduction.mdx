## What is Outception?

Outception is a **live news wall** aggregating headlines from 800+ sources,
combined with **pay-to-promote** placements: anyone can pay to feature a
promotion in the feed. The public news API is open; promotion payments are
handled externally by an external payment provider.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Live News Feed" icon="newspaper">
    A continuously updated wall of headlines aggregated from 800+ sources,
    searchable and grouped by category.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pay to Promote" icon="bullhorn">
    Buy a featured slot to surface a promotion as a "Promoted" card in the
    feed, charged through an external payment provider.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
